Safety & De-escalation

General Safety

  • ‚Ä¢ONLY cross when walk sign is GREEN
  • ‚Ä¢If group splits, wait on other side
  • ‚Ä¢Carry tour sign if 10+ people
  • ‚Ä¢Hold doors for accessible tours
  • ‚Ä¢Use elevators in accessible tours (walk around Tesco, no stairs)
  • ‚Ä¢Trust your instincts‚Äîif something feels wrong, remove group from situation

Dealing with Hostile Person

Initial Response:

  • ‚Ä¢ Take deep breath, remain calm
  • ‚Ä¢ Give full attention, maintain eye contact
  • ‚Ä¢ Don't take it personally
  • ‚Ä¢ Use universal greeting

‚úì De-escalation Tactics:

  • ‚Ä¢ Listen actively and respectfully
  • ‚Ä¢ Show empathy, establish rapport
  • ‚Ä¢ Provide choices
  • ‚Ä¢ Speak slowly and controlled
  • ‚Ä¢ Find solutions together
  • ‚Ä¢ Set appropriate limits

‚úó Things to Avoid:

  • ‚Ä¢ Getting too emotionally involved
  • ‚Ä¢ Power struggles or intimidation
  • ‚Ä¢ Saying "I know how you feel"
  • ‚Ä¢ Face-to-face/toe-to-toe posture
  • ‚Ä¢ Touching the person
  • ‚Ä¢ Fake smiling
